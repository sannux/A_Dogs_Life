Grooming

A DOG'S LIFE believes your dog should enjoy a trip to the spa and not fear it! Our staff incorporate a gentle and tender loving care approach to our spa guests. We take our time, make our doggies feel at ease, massage, pet and relax them so they do not fear the groomer. Having a bonding session or just sitting down in staff members lap and having some cookies before a nail trim or bath is not uncommon for our spa clients.

Daycare

A DOG'S LIFE provide you with peace of mind while our experienced Playgroup Leaders lead your dog through a structured day of dog and people socialization, exercise, play, one-on-one care, and a little rest and relaxation with personalized naptimes. Our custom indoor/outdoor daycare facilities are divided into separate play areas for small dogs, medium dogs, and large dogs. All dogs have regular access to play outdoors and can even take a dip in the pooch pool on warm days.

We encourage our human clients to visit our Owners' Lounge to have a cup of coffee or tea and sit down with our trainers to discuss issues regarding their dog, dog behavior or even pick up quick training tips. We believe a well-educated owner is a more effective owner, which leads to a higher quality of life for both owner and dog.

Holiday home

A DOG'S LIFE offers an On-Site Slumber Party combined with daycare for one price so you can have peace of mind knowing that they are having a great time while you're away.

Your dog will enjoy a safe, closely supervised indoor/outdoor playground with other dogs during the day. At nighttime, your tired pup will slumber in a private kennel equipped with their own toys, water, and a comfortable bed. We even have staff working overnight to make sure that all dogs are sleeping comfortably and soundly. Our overnight attendant is an active position with a variety of tasks, not just someone sleeping on-site overnight. Our overnight attendants regularly check in on our sleeping pups to ensure that all are safe and well.
