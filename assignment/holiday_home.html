Holiday home

A DOG'S LIFE offers an On-Site Slumber Party combined with daycare for one price so you can have peace of mind knowing that they are having a great time while you're away.

Your dog will enjoy a safe, closely supervised indoor/outdoor playground with other dogs during the day. At nighttime, your tired pup will slumber in a private kennel equipped with their own toys, water, and a comfortable bed. We even have staff working overnight to make sure that all dogs are sleeping comfortably and soundly. Our overnight attendant is an active position with a variety of tasks, not just someone sleeping on-site overnight. Our overnight attendants regularly check in on our sleeping pups to ensure that all are safe and well.

One night:

• €70 per dog
• €60 per additional dog

One week:

• €320 per dog
• €300 per additional dog
